Our Story

Building a community where lost items find their way home

Our Mission

The BUBT Lost and Found System is dedicated to helping our university community reunite with lost belongings. We believe that every lost item has an owner who misses it, and every found item deserves to find its way back home.

Community Driven

This system thrives on the participation of our entire BUBT community - students, faculty, and staff. Together, we create a network of support that helps everyone find what they've lost.

Safe and Secure

Your personal information is protected, and our system ensures that only authorized users can access sensitive details. We prioritize privacy and security in all operations.

24/7 Access

Our online platform is available around the clock, allowing you to report lost items or search for found items at any time that's convenient for you.
